Why start in Secondary 1 or 2?

The transition from primary school maths to secondary school maths is bigger than most students expect. Algebra, geometry, and number theory arrive all at once — and gaps formed in Sec 1 compound into bigger problems by the time O-Levels come around.

Students who get the right support early don't just do better in Sec 1 and 2 — they arrive at Sec 3 with the confidence and foundation to tackle A Math with ease, giving them a decisive advantage over their peers.

The earlier a student builds a strong relationship with their tutor, the more personalised and effective the guidance becomes over time. We understand how each student thinks, where they get stuck, and how to explain things in a way that clicks for them.
